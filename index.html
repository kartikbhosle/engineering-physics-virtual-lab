About VirtuLab Platform

VirtuLab bridges the gap between theoretical physics and practical application. By simulating complex optical, electrical, and physical phenomena in real-time, students can conduct precise experiments without the constraints of physical lab hours or equipment availability.

  • ✓ Highly accurate physics engines mapping theoretical equations.
  • ✓ Interactive components replacing fragile physical optics.
  • ✓ Automated data logging and professional graph generation.
